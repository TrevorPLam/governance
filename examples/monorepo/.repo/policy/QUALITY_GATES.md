# /.repo/policy/QUALITY_GATES.md
Quality gates are enforced by governance verification. Hard gates require required artifacts, valid trace logs, completed HITL items, and governance-verify passing. Waiverable gates include coverage, budgets, and warnings, which require an approved waiver with expiration and remediation plan.
